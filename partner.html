// The models

If we decide to build together, this is how partnerships are usually structured. Which one we land on depends on your stage — we work that out with you.

Model 01

Equity only

Best for: Early-stage ideas with no revenue yet.

You bring the domain expertise and distribution. We build the product. No monthly cost — we take 30–40% equity instead. Pure shared upside, zero billing.

Model 02

Retainer + equity

Best for: Ideas with some validation, or partners who want faster commitment from both sides.

A small monthly retainer (NOK 15–25k) covers infrastructure and direct costs. We take 20–35% equity. This is how most of our partnerships are structured.

Model 03

Milestone-vested equity

Best for: Partners who want to stay lean until traction is proven.

Equity is allocated in tranches as milestones are hit — MVP, first paying customers, revenue targets. Retainer optional. Structure agreed upfront.
