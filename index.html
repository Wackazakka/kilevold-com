// How we work
01

You bring the idea

Domain expertise and distribution. You don't need to be technical — you need to know the problem space better than anyone.

02

We build the product

Design, engineering, and AI integration. No hourly invoices, no scope creep. A small monthly retainer (NOK 15–25k) covers infrastructure and direct costs.

03

We grow it together

Equity vests against milestones — typically 20–35% to NordiTech. You own the brand, customers, and domain. We own the upside together.

Building something in media, finance — or anything else?

Payment flows, subscriptions, APIs, AI pipelines — we build almost anything, from the simple to the deeply technical. We partner with founders and domain experts who bring a bold idea and the conviction to see it through. No pitch deck required — just a business we believe in.
